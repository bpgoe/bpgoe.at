OUR MISSION

"Promoting the well-being of Filipino healthcare professionals in Austria, offering crucial support, and encouraging cooperation among members and stakeholders are all ways to empower them. Our goal is to establish a community that fosters both professional and personal development while maintaining the highest standards of healthcare quality and adhering to Austrian laws and regulations."

OUR VISION

"To be the premier association for Filipino medical professionals in Austria, known for promoting their rights, improving their health, and creating a solid support system. We see a community that upholds the greatest standards of moral and legal behavior, flourishes by teamwork, and makes a significant contribution to Austria's healthcare system."
